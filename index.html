Game Info & Privacy

About The Game

Circuit Logic: Mastermind is a puzzle game inspired by "Lights Out".

The Goal: Turn all the lights ON (Green) to restore power to the system. Clicking a cell toggles itself and its adjacent neighbors (Up, Down, Left, Right).

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

  • Data Collection: We store your game progress (Coins, Unlocked Levels) and email in our secure database to allow you to login from different devices.
  • Security: Passwords are encrypted and handled by Firebase Authentication.
